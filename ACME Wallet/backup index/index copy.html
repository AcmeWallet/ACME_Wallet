All cryptos, manage everything

The most profound ideas are often the simplest, and ours has developed from several connected and fundamental beliefs that we put into work.

  • The cryptocurrency user experience needs to be simplified for users.
  • We believe this can be done from a single interface.
  • Users should be rewarded for keeping the assets in their own custody.
This is our vision, and we want to take some time to share.
The Problem
Cryptocurrency users expose themselves to multiple risks and obstacles when owning, holding and trading

  • Moving money around wallets and to different exchanges and wed-interfaces involves multiple security risks.
  • It makes it difficult to keep track of one’s assets and provides a poor user experience.
  • Multiple wallets are needed to hold coins from different blockchains. Each wallet adds complexity to the user experience and reduces security.
The Solution
So, we decided to fix DeFi. Imagine being able to stake tokens in your own wallet, receiving rewards and managing your assets from any chain, all in one place. The coins never leave your custody.

The mantra “Not your keys, not your coins” has been lost somewhat in the rush to send coins to smart contracts in return for rewards. ACME wallet aims to eliminate this by providing an in-wallet rewards for retention of coins. Projects benefit from the incentive-based lock-up of those tokens, and users benefit through asset appreciation, security and ease of use.
The Token
The $ACME token is the native token of the ecosystem. The reward is given to anyone holding tokens in their wallet. Transaction fees fund platform growth and the reward system. As the user base grows, so will the rewards. The platform is token-agnostic: any project will be able to integrate and ensure that holing their tokens is incentivised through $ACME rewards.
Partnerships
We have partnered with Bit Alliance DAO and Reflect Finance , so at launch, users will be rewarded for holding RFI and BPT in their ACME wallet. Any RFI or BPT holders are eligible for this. Just create a wallet and put your tokens in it to experience the first ever self-custodial staking experience in DeFi. It really is a crypto purist’s dream.
Integration
Any project will be able to integrate with the ACME wallet, so that their users can deposit tokens and experience custodial staking. Projects have a massive incentive to do this, as holders will be rewarded based on a time-weighted holding calculation, so the longer you hold, the better the returns. It keeps a user’s tokens entirely liquid, while reducing sell pressure.
